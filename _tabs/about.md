# About Cybersecurityjunior

Welcome to Cybersecurityjunior! This is where I document my journey into cybersecurity, sharing what I learn along the way.

From beginner tips and career transitions to advanced homelabs, I'm building this resource to help others who are starting their cybersecurity journey.

## My Journey

I'm transitioning into cybersecurity and documenting everything I learn. Whether it's setting up my first home lab, studying for certifications, or building security tools, you'll find it here.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Home Lab Guides**: Step-by-step tutorials on building cybersecurity labs
- **Career Tips**: Advice on transitioning into cybersecurity roles
- **Technical Tutorials**: Practical guides on security tools and techniques
- **Project Walkthroughs**: Deep dives into security projects I've built

Stay curious, keep learning, and join me on this cybersecurity journey!
